Acro Yoga

Acro Yoga is a combination of Yoga & Acrobatics in which two or more individuals create spectacular figures with their bodies off the ground. It takes flexibility, balance, strength and lots of trust.

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga derives from kundalini, defined in tantra as energy that lies within the body, frequently at the navel or the base of the spine.

Prenatal Yoga

Prenatal yoga is a type of yoga designed for pregnant women. Yoga is intended to create a balance between emotional, mental, physical, and spiritual dimensions.

Hot Yoga

The first form of hot yoga was Bikram Yoga, founded by Bikram Choudhury in the 1970s.

Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is slow-paced style of yoga as exercise, incorporating principles of traditional Chinese medicine, with asanas that are held for longer periods of time than in other styles.
